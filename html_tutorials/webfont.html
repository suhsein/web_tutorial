I'm Thinking of Ending Things.

Directed by Charlie Kaufman. With Jesse Plemons, Jessie Buckley, Toni Collette, David Thewlis. Full of misgivings, a young woman travels with her new boyfriend to his parents' secluded farm. Upon arriving, she comes to question everything she thought she knew about him, and herself.

Aided by stellar performances from Jessie Buckley and Jesse Plemons, I'm Thinking of Ending Things finds writer-director Charlie Kaufman grappling with the human condition as only he can.
